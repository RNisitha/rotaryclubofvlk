Join Us

Rooted In Service

Growing a greener, kinder Velankanni through unity.

Velankanni Green City Rotary Club brings neighbors, professionals, students, faith communities, and service-minded families together under the Rotary spirit of action. Our work connects environmental sustainability with practical community care, from green drives and health access to education support.

Inspired by Velankanni's culture of welcome, the club honors unity across religions and traditions while serving the shared dignity of every person.

Green City Promise Service that begins with the earth and reaches every home.
